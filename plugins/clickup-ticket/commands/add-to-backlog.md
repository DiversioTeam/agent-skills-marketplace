Use your `clickup-ticket` Skill in **add-to-backlog** mode.

Ultra-fast ticket creation to your configured backlog list.
No prompts, no assignee, normal priority.

**Usage:**
```
/clickup-ticket:add-to-backlog "Your idea here"
/clickup-ticket:add-to-backlog "Refactor auth module"
```

**Flags:**
- `--org=<slug>` - Add to different org's backlog
- `--assign` - Assign to yourself

See the SKILL.md for full details.
